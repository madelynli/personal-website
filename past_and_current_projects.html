South Bay Rice Things

This mobile app is an UI design for a Japanese restaurant I worked at last summer called South Bay Rice Things. The restaurant mainly uses delivery services such as Grubhub and DoorDash for customers. However, I noticed that the restaurant lacked efficiency in their system of using tablets to alert the employees of online orders. Thus, I became inspired to create an app to help them. I used Figma to create the UI design and Photoshop to edit food images.

Little Paws

Little Paws provides pet care after school in the South Bay of LA. Run by a group of students, the earnings go to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society. To better communicate with our clients, I used Figma to design a website.
From research, ideation, and to UI design and implementation, I designed the Little Paws webpage to display pet care staff, services, rates, operation time and contact information. I also created an input form to collect schedule book info from users. I chose a card-style and grid-based layout to help users access the contents easily. The typography hierarchy highlights the important information. I chose the primary color to be a light grayish orange color. This warm, low-contrast color gently welcomes viewers into the website. I designed a little paw image in an ellipse and heart shape on Figma, then inserted it into text for a simple logo. I designed it holistically by creating branding, logos and a color scheme to attract new clients.
